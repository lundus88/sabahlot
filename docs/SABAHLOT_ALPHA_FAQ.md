# SabahLot Alpha FAQ

Purpose: provide simple answers for Alpha users, Facebook respondents, and early testers.

## 1. What is SabahLot?

SabahLot is a preliminary land workflow web app. It helps users record land location, draw an approximate area on a map, calculate estimated area/perimeter, save records, and produce preliminary output for early reference.

## 2. Is SabahLot an official survey plan?

No. SabahLot output is preliminary only. It is not an official survey plan and not a certified survey document.

## 3. Can SabahLot confirm land boundary?

No. SabahLot cannot confirm legal boundary. Boundary must be verified by the relevant authority, licensed surveyor, or professional adviser.

## 4. Can SabahLot prove ownership?

No. SabahLot is not proof of land ownership and does not confirm land title status.

## 5. Can SabahLot be used for subdivision approval?

No. SabahLot can only help with preliminary planning and record preparation. It is not subdivision approval and does not replace official application processes.

## 6. Who can use SabahLot Alpha?

Suitable early users:
- landowner
- family of landowner
- kampung/community user
- technical mapping user
- listing partner candidate
- people willing to give honest feedback

## 7. Why is the Alpha link not public?

SabahLot is still in Controlled Alpha. Access is limited to reduce risk, protect reputation, and allow feedback to be reviewed carefully.

## 8. Is my land data public?

Alpha data should be treated as private-by-default. Users should not share sensitive land documents or personal information publicly.

## 9. What should I test?

Please test:
- open the map
- draw a polygon
- save a land record
- load the record again
- export preliminary output
- import KML / GeoJSON / CSV if available
- read the disclaimer

## 10. What feedback is useful?

Useful feedback includes:
- what worked
- what failed
- what was confusing
- what was useful
- what should be improved
- whether the disclaimer was clear
- whether you would use this service again

## Standard Disclaimer

SabahLot output is for preliminary reference only. It is not an official survey plan, not a certified boundary plan, and must not be used as legal proof of boundary, ownership, approval, subdivision, or land title status. All coordinates, boundaries, and areas must be verified by the relevant authority, licensed surveyor, or professional adviser before official use.
