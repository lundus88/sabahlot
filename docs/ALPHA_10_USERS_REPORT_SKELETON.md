## Alpha Objective

The objective of this Controlled Alpha is to test whether selected users can understand and use SabahLot for preliminary land record workflow, including map display, polygon drawing, area estimation, saving land records, importing/exporting basic files, and understanding the preliminary nature of the output.

## Risk and Disclaimer Understanding

Required understanding:
- SabahLot is not an official survey plan.
- SabahLot is not certified boundary proof.
- SabahLot is not ownership proof.
- SabahLot is not official subdivision approval.
- SabahLot is not confirmation of land title status.
- All output is preliminary and must be verified by relevant authority, licensed surveyor or professional adviser.
